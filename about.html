About Us

Supporting independent coffee culture since 2025

What is indi.cafe?

indi.cafe is a free tool that helps you find the nearest independent coffee shops to your location. We believe the best coffee experiences come from local, independent cafes — not national chains — and we built this site to make it easier to discover them.

How it works

Enter your postcode, town, or share your current location, and indi.cafe searches for independent coffee shops nearby using the Google Places API. Results are filtered to exclude national and international chains, so you only see genuinely local cafes.

We show you distance, estimated travel time, opening hours, ratings, and other useful details — everything you need to find your next favourite spot.

Our mission

Independent coffee shops are the heart of local communities. They offer quality, character, and connection that no chain can replicate. Our goal is simple: help people find and support their local independent cafes, one cup at a time.
